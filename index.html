Features

This is an E-Banking Application. Some of the features are decribed below.

Transfer Funds

Payment can be transferred from your current account to to savings account and vice versa. Payment can also be converted to Euros and US Dollars.

Make Payments

Payments can be made from one account to another. Payment can also be converted to eEros and US Dollars.

Print Transcation Statements

Transcation statements can be printed to and from any date of choosing.

Multi-Application Viewing

This application can be viewed on desktop and mobile applications.
